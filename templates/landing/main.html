FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MaternalCare.

Is my health data secure on the platform?

Yes, MaternalCare is fully HIPAA compliant and uses industry-leading security standards to protect your health information. All data is encrypted and we never sell your information to third parties.

Can I connect with my existing healthcare provider?

Absolutely! You can invite your current healthcare providers to connect to your profile. We also offer integration with major hospital and clinic EHR systems for seamless data sharing with your consent.

When should I start using MaternalCare?

You can start using MaternalCare at any point in your pregnancy journey. However, starting early provides the most comprehensive tracking and care support throughout your pregnancy.

Does MaternalCare offer postpartum support?

Yes, our platform provides comprehensive postpartum support, including tracking recovery milestones, mental health resources, breastfeeding support, and baby development tracking for the first year.

How do the virtual consultations work?

Our virtual consultations are conducted through our secure in-app video platform. Premium users receive two free specialist consultations and can book additional sessions at preferred rates. All consultations are with licensed healthcare professionals specialized in maternal care.
