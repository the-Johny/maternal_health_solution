Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I change my plan during my pregnancy?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll have immediate access to all new features. When downgrading, changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of our Premium plan for all new users. No credit card required to start your trial.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. Annual subscriptions are eligible for a prorated refund after the 30-day period.

Is my insurance accepted?

MaternalCare is covered by select insurance providers. Check our insurance page for a current list of participating providers or contact your insurance company directly.

How are specialist consultations billed?

Premium users receive 2 free specialist consultations per month. Additional consultations can be booked at preferred rates. Provider plan users have unlimited access to specialist consultations for their patients.
